MBA IN FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

BIBS offers a full-time 2 year course in MBA in Finance Management with a special focus on finance. MBA in Finance Management aims to equip students with the skills that are required by modern-day banking and finance institutions by helping the students to develop competencies across four dimensions—domain, technology, application and customer-service. BIBS - one of the best MBA colleges in Kolkata placement wise, provides core training along with key certifications and this course aims at making students skilled and completely ready to step into the world of business and finance.

Highlights

Affiliation : Regular Full - Time MBA degree affiliated to Vidyasagar University, A NAAC accredited, W.B. State Government University, recognized by UGC, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India. of India.

Exclusive International Certification: For the first time in West Bengal, BIBS has tied-up with CISI, London to provide the Investment Operations Certificate, a globally accepted curriculum endorsed and preferred by major financial institutions across more than 100 countries., and the best course in MBA finance management.

The CISI Jobs Advantage: The Investment Operations Certificate provides flourishing careers in Financial Operations for crucial roles in the Risk Management and Compliance departments in Financial Services organizations and banks. As an Investment Operations Executive, you can get alluring job roles within banks and financial businesses which are not sales ( derived or physical).

Apprenticeship: Confirmed paid Apprenticeship for all students after 11 months of core training in Financial Management in the best companies in the Finance Industry. Companies like PwC, SBI MF, Aditya Birla MF, HDFC AMC, HDFC Bank, ICICI Securities and many more have already hired MBA-PGPFM students.

Certifications: in Finance with BFSI SSC, under the NSDC, an initiative of the Government of India. This tie - up will provide training, mentorship & certifications in the fields of Banking & Finance.

Advantage CISI

Global Connect

The Investment Operations Certificate is now taken in over 50 countries worldwide and is the principal certification for administration and operations employees to demonstrate their knowledge and skills.

With more than 40,000 members across 110 different countries, makes CISI the largest professional institute representing people working within the industry. This means that it is well respected globally as a benchmark of quality financial knowledge at par with requirements of large organizations.

International Placement Opportunities

The Investment Operations Certificate is considered by Banks and Financial Institutions globally to ascertain the quality of their Financial Operations staff. This gives you a globally valid and duly accepted specialization that will open doors for you anywhere in the world

And with CISI standards being accepted across the world (including India), you will have the CISI brand value to help you with Internships as well as final placement assistance across these countries.

As an Investment Operations Executive, you can get alluring job roles within banks and financial businesses like:

  • Compliance Officer
  • Compliance Manager
  • Compliance Auditor
  • Risk Officer
  • Risk Analyst
  • Risk Manager
  • Risk Control Manager
And many more

And Guess what? These are all supervisory, analytical and client interaction roles which do not involve physical or derived Sales jobs.

A globally accepted and valued Certification

Banks, Trading houses and Financial Services companies across the world prefer Investment Operations Certificate holders for their Financial Operations and Risk and Quality Control Verticals.

Like they say, you can make the world your oyster

World Class Training by CISI

The CISI works in partnership with lots of high-level trainers and practitioners, who are well known within the industry and around the world. Add to that the highly efficient and qualified faculty team at BIBS, means that not only will you have gained an impressive qualification, but you will have done it at an impressive institution as well.

You will be trained by the best … to be the best

Fully Immersive Knowledge Portal

You get access to a fully loaded Knowledge Portal with customized content based on region and job relevance

With assignment and practice modules, the knowledge portal guides your core learning with immersive content that makes your learning more real life and hence exciting to pursue
