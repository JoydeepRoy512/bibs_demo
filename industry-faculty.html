Educational Loan Support

Doorway to the career of your dreams!

BIBS offers student loan facilities to students in order to pursue higher education. Scheduled commercial banks are rendering loans to students in the form of credit under the auspices of BIBS institute.

Educational Loans are provided at Post-Graduation for several streams. Here at BIBS, we strive to facilitate quality education for every student to pursue the career of their dreams despite financial barriers.

Student loans will be approbated post-admission, and loan allowances will be paid by the banks directly to the institute. Our admission department will provide all the necessary assistance relating to your educational loan regarding documentation and formalities.

FAQS

1. How is a student loan repaid?
-
A: Student loan repayment (EMI) starts after the disbursement of the loan in case of NBFC (can be within 15 days) and in case of Nationalised Bank /Private Bank, EMI starts after 2 years and even students can avail 1 year of the moratorium period.
2. What are the qualifications for a student loan?
+
A: The eligibility criteria for a student loan as are follows:
  • Student should be a resident of India
  • Must be aged between 18-35 years
  • Student must have a co-applicant to apply for the loan
  • Must have an educational qualification from a recognized School/College/University
  • The co-applicant must have a regular source of income
  • Should possess a good academic record
3. What are the documents required for a student loan?
+
  • Passport Size photo Of Student and Co-Borrower
  • Co-Borrower Address proof
  • Student PAN
  • Prospectus/Certificate (from the competent authority of the institution) wherein charges like
    Admission Fee, Examination Fee, etc. are mentioned
  • Last Exam Document
  • ITR
4. What are the expenses covered by education loans?
+
A: The following are the expense covered by Educational loans:
  • Tuition fees of the institution
  • Library/Exam fees
  • Books/ Uniforms
Student Credit Card

Student Credit Card Scheme

BIBS is offering credit cards for student education issued by The Government of West Bengal. The West Bengal Student Credit Card Scheme at BIBS allows students to pursue their career goals without worrying about financial limitations.

  • The credit capacity of the credit card is 10 lacs.
  • The tenure for the loan is up to 15 years
  • The upper age limit is 40 years
  • The interest rate amounts to 4%

FAQS

1. What are the documents required for a student credit card?
+
A: Following are the documents required for a student credit card:
  • Application Form
  • Identity Proof (Aadhar/ PAN/ Voter ID card)
  • Proof of Residence
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Self Declaration Form/ Letter
2. What is the Eligibility to Apply?
+
  • Must be a resident of West Bengal for at least 10 years
  • Student must not be above 40 years of age
  • Student must be above or in 10th standard
  • The loan must be repaid within 15 years of employment
  • Student must have an educational qualification from a recognized school/college
3. Do you need a guarantor for a student credit card?
+
A: No, it is not necessary to have a guarantor to apply for a student credit card. The state takes care of your expenses and acts as a guarantor for your education loan.
